FAQ's

Frequently Asked Questions


How long will it take to get my order?

We aim to deliver your order within 3-5 working days. However,during peak seasons, it may take longer.

How do I track my order??

You will be emailed every step of the way, with shipping and tracking updates along the way.

What happens if no one is home to receive the flowers?

If no one is available, we will attempt to leave the flowers in a safe place. If this is not possible, we will contact the recipient to arrange a suitable delivery time.

How do I take care of my flowers?

We include flower food along with care instructions with every delivery.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery as well as eft payments via our website.
