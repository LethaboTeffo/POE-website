About Us

Welcome to Pixy Blooms.


BRIEF HISTORY.
Pixy Blooms was founded on January 30th, 2025, by Lethabo Teffo. With a vision to bring a touch of floral magic to every day, Lethabo established Pixy Blooms as
a unique floral service. Understanding the power of flowers to transform spaces and uplift spirits, the business was created to cater to those who appreciate the beauty
and artistry of floral arrangements.

MISSION.
Our mission is to cultivate connections and brighten lives by providing unique, high-quality floral arrangements that reflect our customers' individual needs and personalities.
Through the language of flowers, we aim to be a source of joy and inspiration.

VISION.
To be the go-to floral destination, recognized for our creativity, personalized service, and commitment to bringing a touch of magic to every occasion. We envision a world
where the beauty of flowers enriches everyday life.
